## ◈ About

<img align="right" width="340" src="https://github-readme-stats.vercel.app/api?username=tharunstryker&show_icons=true&theme=tokyonight&bg_color=070714&border_color=7C3AED&title_color=7C3AED&icon_color=00D4FF&text_color=FFFFFF&ring_color=7C3AED&hide_border=false" />

First-year AI & Data Science engineer and solo founder. Building **Neeti by Aevra** — a production-grade dual-product SaaS platform for Indian students — entirely on Android via Termux. No laptop. No framework. No build step.

Neeti comprises **CareerKit** (five AI career tools: resume, cover letter, LinkedIn bio, cold email, interview prep) and **CardStudio** (a modular interactive greeting card engine with gates, animations, music, and hosted share links). Every layer — product, frontend, backend, AI pipeline, security, billing — is sole-authored.

The stack is intentionally minimal by constraint and by philosophy: vanilla HTML/CSS/JS on Vercel edge, Supabase backend with full RLS, Cloudinary for card media, and a four-provider AI fallback chain with five rotating Gemini keys.

**Open To:**
- AI/ML freelance contracts
- Early-stage technical collaboration
- Open-source contributions in AI tooling
- Remote engineering roles (post-graduation)

## ◈ Experience

**Founder & Full-Stack Engineer** · **Naeris** *(Aevra Studio → Neeti)*
`Jan 2026 — Present`

Solo-built a production SaaS from zero — brand identity, frontend architecture, backend schema, AI pipeline, billing logic, and full security audit — during Year 1 of engineering, on a mobile device.

- Designed and shipped Supabase schema: 5 tables, RLS across all, 2 live migrations, signup trigger, atomic credit operations
- Built 4-stage AI fallback chain with 5-key Gemini rotation and premium queue bypass across 5 CareerKit tools
- Engineered two-cookie admin authentication (`na_sid` HttpOnly + `neeti_admin` client-readable) with non-guessable route and `auth-guard.js` middleware
- Conducted and patched a full security audit: 4 high/critical issues resolved including credit bypass, global scope exploitation, and static engine bundle leaking premium code
- Implemented `_buildEFILES(cfg)` — dynamic per-tier asset bundling ensuring premium engine code is never sent to free users
- Integrated Cloudinary for premium card media (voice, photo, BG music, BG image) with daily cron cleanup of expired assets
- Built complete CardStudio engine v2: registry pattern, 5 gate types, 4 themes, atmosphere module, 2 transition types, 12 letter components
